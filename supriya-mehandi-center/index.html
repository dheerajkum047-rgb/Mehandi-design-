About Us

Supriya Mehandi Center — where traditional art meets modern design

Located in Gaddupur, Shantipur, Prayagraj, Supriya Mehandi Center has been serving brides, festive celebrations and special occasions with mehandi, saree embroidery and custom dress designing for years. Every design is handcrafted with care and precision, so your special moments stay memorable.

  • Bridal and party mehandi by experienced artists
  • Premium embroidery — zardozi, resham, mirror work and more
  • Custom dress designing — lehenga, Anarkali, Indo-western
  • Home service booking also available
  • UPI, card and cash — easy payment options
